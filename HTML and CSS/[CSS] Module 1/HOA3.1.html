Who is TWICE?

TWICE is a South Korean girl group formed by JYP Entertainment through the 2015 reality show "Sixteen." They officially debuted on October 20, 2015, with the extended play (EP) The Story Begins. The group is composed of nine members.

The Members

The group consists of Nayeon, Jeongyeon, Momo, Sana, Jihyo, Mina, Dahyun, Chaeyoung, and Tzuyu. They are known for their strong teamwork and diverse charms.
