The Group

Formed by JYP Entertainment through the reality show "Sixteen," TWICE debuted on October 20, 2015, with the EP *The Story Begins*.

The Fandom

Our official fandom name is ONCE. We share a deep bond, and the official colors are Apricot and Neon Magenta.
